Somali Rice and Goat Meat Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb goat meat, cubed
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add goat meat, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until meat is browned.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Rinse rice and add it to the pot, stirring to coat with the meat and spices.
  6. Add enough water to cover the rice by about an inch. Bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked and water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Somali Rice and Goat Meat!
